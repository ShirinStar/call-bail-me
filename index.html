CALLBAIL.ME

Callbail.me allows to store emergency contacts and local bail funds, to notify them in case of a need.

You will be able to SMS with detailed information of your last location and time saw, or make a quick call with the click of a button.

You need to:
1) find the bail fund neer you by location.
2) mark a contact emergency from your personal contacts you would like to SMS if something gets wrong.

* While you can choose to store one or the other, please make sure to store this information before you get out to protest, to best protect you and your contacts.
